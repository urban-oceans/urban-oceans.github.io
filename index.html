Urban Oceans - Pelagic Ecosystems Lab UBC

About Our Research on Urban Oceans

In 2023, the Pelagic Ecosystems Lab launched a new research direction to understand how urban environments interact with the coastal ocean, influence ecosystem health, and shape biogeochemical processes. Our work focuses on the Vancouver region as a model urban–marine interface.

Read the project announcement

Current Research Questions

  • What is the organic matter and inorganic nutrient content of stormwater, urban rivers, and other urban inputs to the coastal ocean?
  • What biogeochemical signatures do urban inputs leave in the nearshore ocean?
  • What is the spatial footprint of urban material inputs in coastal waters?
  • What is the fate and transformation of urban-derived materials once they enter the ocean?
  • How do urban inputs affect organism health, food webs, and the ecosystem services they support?
  • How does urbanization influence marine biodiversity?
